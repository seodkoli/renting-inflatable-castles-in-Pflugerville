About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, United States, with a little part in Williamson Area. The populace was 65,191 at the 2020 demographics. Pflugerville is a suburban area of Austin and part of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was named after the original German settlers who farmed the location; Pflüger implies "plowman".
