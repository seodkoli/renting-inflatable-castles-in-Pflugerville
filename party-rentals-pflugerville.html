About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, USA, with a small section in Williamson County. The populace was 65,191 at the 2020 demographics. Pflugerville is a residential area of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Location. It was called after the initial German settlers who farmed the location; Pflüger suggests "plowman".
