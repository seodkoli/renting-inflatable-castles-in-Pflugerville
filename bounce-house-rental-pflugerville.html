About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, United States, with a tiny part in Williamson Region. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and part of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Location. It was named after the original German settlers who farmed the location; Pflüger indicates "plowman".
