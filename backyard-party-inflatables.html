About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, United States, with a small portion in Williamson Region. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 demographics. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Location. It was named after the original German settlers who farmed the area; Pflüger indicates "plowman".
