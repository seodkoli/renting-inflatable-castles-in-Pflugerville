About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis County, Texas, United States, with a little part in Williamson Region. The populace was 65,191 at the 2020 demographics. Pflugerville is a suburb of Austin and component of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was named after the original German inhabitants who farmed the area; Pflüger means "plowman".
