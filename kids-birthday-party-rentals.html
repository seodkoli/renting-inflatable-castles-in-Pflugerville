About Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville (FLOO-gÉ™É™ r-vil) is a city in Travis Area, Texas, USA, with a small section in Williamson Area. The population was 65,191 at the 2020 census. Pflugerville is a residential area of Austin and part of the Austin–-- Round Rock–-- Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was called after the initial German inhabitants that farmed the location; Pflüger means "plowman".
